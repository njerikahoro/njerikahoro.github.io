My Journey: A Personal Autobiography

Introduction

My name is Kahoro Njeri Evelyne a final year student at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology pursuing Bachelor of Science in Business Information and Technology. The purpose of this presentation is to analyze my autobiography simply my life up to this point.

Early Years

I was born in a small village in Kiambu County. Growing up in the quaint town of Maplewood, nestled amidst rolling hills and verdant landscapes, I was surrounded by the warmth of family and the simplicity of rural life. My childhood days were spent exploring the woods, climbing trees, and chasing fireflies on summer evenings. It was within the embrace of this close-knit community that I learned the values of honesty, integrity, and compassion that would shape the person I am today

Family and Background

I was born in a family of 4 two girls two boys and I have both of my parents. Growing up with both my parents and siblings is the best gift I ever received. I always had people to look up to for moral, physical and any other support I would ever need. Holidays are important days in our household since they bring us together.
